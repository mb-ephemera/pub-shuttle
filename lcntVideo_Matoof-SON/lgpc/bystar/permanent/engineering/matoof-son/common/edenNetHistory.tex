\begin{itemize}
  \item T-Mobile (Bellevue, Washington) deployed Eden Rock Communications' (Bothell,
    Washington) Eden-NET Self-Organizing Network (SON) solution nationwide during the
    2013–2014 timeframe.
  \item Nokia Networks acquired Eden Rock Communications in July 2015, integrating
    Eden-NET into its portfolio alongside Nokia's iSON Manager to create a consolidated
    multi-vendor SON solution.
  \item With deployments spanning more than a decade, Eden-NET has become a proven
    multi-vendor SON platform trusted by major operators including T-Mobile.
\end{itemize}
